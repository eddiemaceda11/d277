Arizona History

  • 48th state of the US
  • Population of 7,151,502
  • Capital is Phoenix
  1. Arizona is home to 22 national parks and monuments
  2. There are more than 100 wineries and 22 varietals of wine produced in Arizona
  3. Arizona's Sonoran Desert is the only place on earth where the iconic saguaro cactus grows
  4. The Arizona Trail is 800 miles long and is dedicated to hiking, mountain biking, horseback riding and cross-country skiing
  5. The Grand Canyon National Park is one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, and the only one located within the United States.
